This web application prototype was build as part of my Master's thesis in Digital Humanities on the application of gamification to Digital Cultural Heritage Collections.

My goals are to combine gamification and crowdsourcing to enhance users' contribution to the Belgian postcards collection of the KU Leuven University Library and to make its discovery more enjoyable.

Since my programming abilities are still limited, the web application prototype presents many defects and should be improved.
Therefore, any feedback is more than welcome.
