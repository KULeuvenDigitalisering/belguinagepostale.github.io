Bé(l)guinage Postale is a crowdsourcing and gamification project prototype based on the Postcard Collection hold by KU Leuven University Library. Bé(l)guinage Postale invites the public to transcribe Belgian picture postcards greetings while playing.

Helping and learning while playing

Discover the history of the Flemish Béguinages while playing puzzle and quiz games and help with the transcription of old Belgian picture postcards. You are asked to complete a four levels game, where you will have to collect points and prove your guessing and transcription abilities.
